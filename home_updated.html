About MTIEAT

Established in 2010 by Holy Cross Educational Society, Palamaner, MTIEAT aims to empower rural youth with quality engineering education. It is located near Palamaner surrounded by lush greenery and natural landscapes.

Vision

To promote excellence in Engineering Education, Research, and Consultancy with values, skills, and knowledge to shape responsible citizens.

Mission

  • Develop professionalism through innovative teaching methods.
  • Promote ethical values through research and consultancy.
  • Maintain high standards in engineering education.
  • Enhance intellectual skills of stakeholders.

Program Outcomes (POs)

  • PO1: Apply engineering knowledge to solve complex problems.
  • PO2: Analyze problems using research and mathematics.
  • PO3: Design solutions with societal and environmental awareness.
  • PO4: Conduct experiments and research for valid conclusions.
  • PO5: Use modern tools with awareness of limitations.
  • PO6: Assess societal and health impacts of engineering decisions.
  • PO7: Understand and promote sustainable development.
  • PO8: Commit to professional ethics.
  • PO9: Work effectively in teams and diverse environments.
  • PO10: Communicate effectively in engineering contexts.
  • PO11: Apply management principles in projects.
  • PO12: Engage in lifelong learning.
